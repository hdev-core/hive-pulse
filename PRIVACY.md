# Privacy Policy for HivePulse

**Last updated: December 16, 2025**

Thank you for using HivePulse. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Information We Collect

HivePulse is designed to be a client-side application. We do **not** collect or store any of your personal data on our servers.

*   **Hive Account Information:** Your Hive username is stored locally on your computer using the `chrome.storage` API to keep you logged in to the chat and to fetch your account statistics. Your private keys are never accessed or stored by HivePulse. All transactions are signed using Hive Keychain.
*   **Chat Messages:** Chat messages are sent directly to and from the Ecency API. We do not store your messages on our servers.
*   **Browsing Data:** The `activeTab` permission is used to read the URL of the currently active tab to enable the frontend switching feature. This information is used only when you interact with the extension and is not stored or transmitted.

## How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide the functionality of the HivePulse extension. We do not use your information for any other purpose, such as tracking or advertising.

## Information We Share

We do not share your personal information with any third parties.

## Cookies

We use cookies to authenticate with the Ecency API. These cookies are only used for the chat functionality and are not used for tracking.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us through our GitHub repository.
